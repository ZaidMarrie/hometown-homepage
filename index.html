Johannesburg

Johannesburg, also known as the Jozi, Joburg, or “The City of Gold”.

Johannesburg is the largest city in South Africa, and is one of the 100 largest urban areas in the world.

Gold Reef City Theme park

The Gold Reef City Theme Park is great place for a day out with family or friends. The theme park has many attractions such as water rides, roller coasters, and the famous gold reef city casino.

Johannesburg zoo

The Johannesburg Zoo has about 2000 animals of 320 different species. A visit to the zoo could be a pleasurable way to entertain or learn.
